##  Caldo Verde

Total time:

INGREDIENTS: Caldo Verde: - 3 pcs of chorizo sausage, sliced - 2 whole
potatoes, chopped - 1 whole onion, chopped - 8 cloves of garlic,
sliced - chicken stock, as needed - 1 bunch of kale, roughly chopped -
Pepper INSTRUCTIONS: - Start by chopping your chorizo into small discs,
and fry them in a hot pan. Allow the oil from the chorizo to render, and
wait til the pieces are crisp before removing from the container. Set
aside for later. - Into the pot with chorizo oil goes the chopped
onions, potatoes, and garlic, and saute until softened. - Pour chicken
stock and allow the soup to simmer to soften the potatoes, before going
blending to create a rich broth. It should have a thick consistency. -
Add back in your chorizo along with some chopped Kale, Serve hot and
enjoy!
