##  Linssi-pekonikeitto

Total time:

Ingredients 150g Smoked Bacon Lardons 1 Yellow or White Onion- finely
chopped 2 Sticks of Celery- finely chopped 1 Large Carrot- finely
chopped 5 Garlic Cloves- chopped 220g French Lentils (Lentilles du Puy
OR Green/ Brown Lentils) 1.5L High quality Chicken Stock 1 Can of
Tomatoes- diced or whole plum (crush with your hands) 2 Bay Leaves 1 Tsp
dried Herbs de Province 1 Tsp Apple Cider Vinegar Fresh parsley to
garnish Peppery olive oil to drizzle Method 1) Fry the bacon in a
generous drizzle of olive oil until golden brown. Add the chopped
vegetables and garlic and cook for 5-8 minutes until softened. 2) Add
the lentils and the rest of the ingredients into the bowl and bring to a
gentle simmer. Cook for 50 minutes until the lentils have lost some of
their bite. 3) Blend 1/4 of the mixture using a hand blender or a food
processor. Serve with a generous drizzle of peppery olive oil and fresh
parsley.
