##  Parmentier de Poisson

Total time:

4 russet potatoes 4Tbsp salted butter 3 egg yolks Salt & White Pepper 1
large leek 2 shallots 1/2 bulb fennel 1lb fish of your choice 2Tbsp AP
flour 2Tbsp butter 1Tbsp <span class="citation"
cites="algaecookingclub">@algaecookingclub oil 1Tbsp chopped dill
1Tbsp chopped parsley 1/2Tbsp chopped tarragon 1.5 cups milk 1 cup cream
Zest 1/2 lemon Salt & White pepper to taste 1/4tsp grated nutmeg -Saute
veg until leeks are tender -Add flour and cook for 2 minutes -Slowly
whisk in milk, once incorporated whisk in cream -Cook for 3 minutes
until thickened -Add fish, zest, herbs, stir in well and turn off heat
-Make mashed potato as seen -Assemble and bake at 375f for 35 minutes or
until golden brown -Enjoy
