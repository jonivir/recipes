##  Camping Breakfast Sandwich

Total time: 25 minuuttia

  [Original](https://theadventurebite.com/camping-breakfast-sandwich/),
  
- **Categories**: [Pääruoka](#category-pääruoka)
- **Ingredients**:
  - 4 Bay’s Sourdough English Muffins
  - 2 cups leftover BBQ pulled pork
  - 4 eggs
  - 4 slices cheddar cheese
  - Butter to crisp up the muffins
- **Instructions**:
  - In a medium cast iron saucepan, crisp up the english muffins with a
    bit of butter until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  - Reheat leftover pork in skillet and set aside.
  - Fry eggs.
  - Fill english muffin with ½ cup pulled pork, runny egg and a slice of
    cheddar cheese. Wrap in foil and place over fire until cheese is
    melted.
  - Serve!
