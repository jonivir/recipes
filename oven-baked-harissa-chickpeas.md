##  OVEN BAKED HARISSA CHICKPEAS

Total time:

A rich, smoky, and spicy chickpea dish that’s perfect for dipping,
scooping, or just devouring straight from the pan. Slow-roasted tomatoes
and peppers bring sweetness, chickpeas add a hearty bite, and a swirl of
hummus and harissa oil takes it to the next level. Served up with
golden, buttered sourdough for the ultimate comfort bite. Ingredients:
For the Chickpeas: 2 tbsp olive oil 200g cherry tomatoes 1 red romano
pepper 2 tbsp rose harissa paste 1 x 400g tin chopped tomatoes 1 x 700g
jar chickpeas (with liquid) Salt, pepper & sugar to taste For Serving:
Smooth hummus Harissa oil Chopped parsley Pine nuts Sourdough toast
(buttered with plant-based spread) How to Make It: 1️⃣ Roast the Veg:
Slice the romano pepper, then toss with cherry tomatoes, olive oil, salt
& pepper in an ovenproof pan. Roast at 220ºC for 15 mins. 2️⃣ Build the
Base: Stir in harissa, chopped tomatoes & chickpeas (with their juices).
Return to the oven for 25-30 mins until thick and jammy. 3️⃣ Finish &
Serve: Swirl hummus over the top, drizzle with harissa oil, scatter with
pine nuts & parsley. Serve hot with buttered sourdough.
