##  Jalapeño-Spiked Bourbon Julep

Total time: 35 minuuttia

Just in time for the Kentucky Derby, the state’s classic cocktail gets a
kick from homemade jalapeño simple syrup.


  
  [Original](https://www.countryliving.com/food-drinks/recipes/a4261/jalapeno-spiked-bourbon-julep-recipe-clv0513/),
  
- **Categories**: [Juomat](#category-juomat)
- **Ingredients**:
  - 1 c. sugar
  - 2 jalapeño peppers, coarsely chopped
  - 4 fresh mint leaves, plus 1 sprig for garnish
  - 1 oz. Jalapeño Simple Syrup
  - Crushed Ice
  - 2 1/2 oz. bourbon
  - Club Soda
  - 1 very thinly sliced jalapeño round, seeded
- **Instructions**:
  - Make simple syrup: In a small saucepan over high heat, combine 1 cup
    water, sugar, and jalapeños. Bring to a boil, stirring to dissolve
    sugar. Remove from heat and let steep 20 minutes.
  - Strain syrup, discard jalapeños, and cool syrup. (Simple syrup can
    be refrigerated, in an airtight container, for up to 6 months.)
  - Make Julep: In a julep cup, pour Jalapeño Simple Syrup and add mint;
    gently bruise leaves with a muddler or wooden spoon. Add enough
    crushed ice to fill two-thirds of the cup. Add bourbon and stir
    gently; then add a bit more crushed ice. Top with a splash of club
    soda. Garnish with mint sprig and jalapeño.
